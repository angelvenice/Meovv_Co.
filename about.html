About Us!

Welcome to Meovv Co. - Where Paws Meet Brews!

Founded in 2025, Meovv Co. was created with a simple vision: to bring together the love of exceptional coffee and the calming presence of our furry companions. As cat lovers, we wanted to build a space where you can enjoy the best of both worlds—delicious brews and the quiet, soothing company of our resident cats.

At Meovv Co., we believe in the beauty of peaceful moments. Whether you're here to savor a freshly brewed coffee, indulge in a delicious pastry, or simply unwind with the calming energy of our friendly cats, we’ve crafted a space that feels just like home.

Join us for a cup, a purr, and the serenity that comes with both. Meovv Co. is more than just a café; it’s a retreat where you can relax, recharge, and enjoy the simple joys of life.
